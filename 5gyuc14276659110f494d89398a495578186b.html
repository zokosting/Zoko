1. Eloqua vs cignasalud:
We decided to run all campaigns from Eloqua instead of from link – mainly because we do not have a well working CMS for cignasalud.es and the campaign tracking would need to be setup from cero basically. That’s why we use Eloqua, there we have the reports already there etc – its more complete to work there. Only very few things are published within the cignasalud directory. We actually wanted to move all campaign pages from cignaslaud.es to Eloqua . Big thing also is we do not have landing page templates within the cignasalud.es system. Should be all either in Eloqua or Drupal.
