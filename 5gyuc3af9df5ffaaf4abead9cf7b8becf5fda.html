Es curioso sentirse un enemigo al que hay que derribar cuando uno solo intenta sobrevivir y seguir adelante sin hundirse en el lodo. Sin éxito.
Cuando uno solo intenta que no le hagan más daño.

I suppose I will always be alone. With no family, no roots. I’ve tried to understand what’s wrong with me.

But honestly I have this feeling that I’ve spent too much efforts trying to comunicate myself, to be understood. With completely no success.
