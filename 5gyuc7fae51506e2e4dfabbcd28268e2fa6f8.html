"CANNIBAL CITY" is headless, is heartless.
"Cannibal City" has no political or social limits it could be communist or post-communist, it could be the former ghetto in a dictatorship or it could be the ramshackle poor areas on the outskirts of big urban conglomerations. It may also be a neglected historical centre in the downtown. You find it on ALL continents except that in some cases the officials in power, or blinkered nationalists, are in a state of self-denial - they simply say "it does not exist". To talk about it implies that one is not 'patriotic'...

Cannibal City starts like a small cancerous growth and soon takes over the whole urban body of the City, suffocates it and devours it from within. It has no rules and no imagination other than the rule of Money, of the Arbitrary, of the Obnoxious.
Cannibal City devours its children, treats its denizens like second-hand citizens, defecates on the doorstep, urinates in public places, defaces the buildings, demolishes the monuments, destroys the Memory. Cannibal City's Constitution is the Pre-Programmed Amnesia.
Cannibal City is the place where the strongest and the meanest rule with complete disregard for the Downtrodden, for Civilization, disregard for Culture, for History, for Memory, disregard for the Sacred, for the Spiritual, for Humanity. The de facto rulers of the great Game change the rules as they go along in order to stay in power for ever for so long as it suits them.
