Fonts
Traditionally, serif fonts are used for body copy (long passages of text), because it's throught that the serifs help the eye move along more quickly.


How to Marry Text and Images

- Textured background (photo) => Try a sans serif (easy to read).
- Use text to fill empty spaces in your photos (‘copy space’).
- Take advantage oflines in your images.
- Try choosing the darkest color from your background image as an alternative to black text.
