1.There must be NO SPACES within a UTM URL

–Why? A Space will potentially break the URL and the part after the space will not be recorded by Google Analytics

–If a space is required place use “_” instead

2.Within the parameter there should be NO SYMBOLS other than “-” or “_”

–Why? Symbols such as a “.” or “?” may send a wrong signal to and affect the ability to land on the page

3.All letters should be LOWERCASE

–Google Analytics is case sensitive

–If we have different case setting, Google would classify it as different sources

4.Never leave cells in yellow empty

– If it is not applicable state "na"
