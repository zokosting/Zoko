1. Have a deliberate approach to building your internal network.
With more than 70,000 colleagues around the world, I guarantee there are people within Cigna you’d benefit from knowing better.
Ask yourself: Who can I learn from? What leaders role model behaviors that I’d like to emulate?
Be bold – don’t be afraid to reach out, introduce yourself, and ask for a meet-and-greet. Most people will be flattered and willing to talk.

2. Build your external network. Look for groups or associations of professionals in roles similar to yours. You can also reach out to individuals in similar roles at other companies, both within health care and elsewhere, to invite a conversation. LinkedIn can be helpful for this.

3. Maintain those bridges. Don’t underestimate the value of former co-workers. Referrals can be powerful ways to naturally strengthen your network and open up opportunities.
